The team

We are a team of Ph.D. students and researchers from Prague computer science universities. We worked on a number of European and local Linked (Open) Data projects involving hundreds of processed datasets.

The following organizations contributed to the development of LinkedPipes ETL:

  • Faculty of Mathematics and Physics, Charles University
  • Faculty of Informatics and Statistics, University of Economics in Prague
  • Faculty of Information Technology, Czech Technical University in Prague
  • NAKIT - National Agency for Communication and Information Technologies
  • Ministry of the Interior of the Czech Republic
  • Semantica.cz

In addition, the development of LinkedPipes ETL was supported by the following projects:

  • OpenBudgets.eu project. OpenBudgets.eu has received funding from the European Union’s H2020 EU research and innovation programme under grant agreement No 645833.
  • Czech Science Foundation (GAČR), grant number 16-09713S.
  • Wikimedia Foundation Project Grant Wikidata & ETL
